Nursing Booklist

The booklist contains all relevant materials that the instructor has deemed that are required to take their course.

  • Title
  • Author
  • Publisher
  • ISBN
  • Price

Financial aid in the form of grants, loans, or scholarships will not be available to use in the Bookstore until September 16th at 1pm. 

The bookstore endeavors to offer books at the most reasonable prices we can. However books are often found for less online. If you are buying your books from a different source, we recommend matching the ISBN from the booklist to be sure that you get the exact same book. Some instructors require books with an online component or access code. When you are buying a book from another location or a used book it may not come with this online component. Be sure to check the requirements as you will need to buy any online required materials in order to complete the course, plus the cost is usually more to purchase them separately. The bookstore often offers online codes and books as a bundle that are less expensive.

Due to the high volume of telephone calls before each term, we are unable to provide ISBN's over the phone. Please reference the booklist to see ISBN's for all course textbooks.
